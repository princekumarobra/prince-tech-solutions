About Prince Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

We are an Aurangabad, Bihar based tech company dedicated to building fast, clean, and affordable digital solutions for local businesses and institutions.

The Story Behind PTS

Hi, I'm Prince Kumar, the founder. My journey started with a simple vision: to make quality web services accessible to everyone, especially local businesses in Bihar that deserve a professional online presence but are often limited by high costs and complex solutions.

I gained deep experience in custom coding and managing high-traffic portals like FastUpdate. This background taught me the value of clean code, security, and above all, speed. Prince Tech Solutions was founded on these principles.

We don't just build websites; we build digital assets that convert visitors into clients, all while focusing on local, transparent, and easy-to-understand support.

**Mission:** Affordable, clean, and fast websites for local businesses.

**Location:** Proudly based in Aurangabad, Bihar, serving clients globally.
