Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find solutions to common queries about our services, payments, and support.

1. Website Development

Website banane mein kitna samay lagega?

Basic websites ke liye 7-10 din. Business/Govt portals ke liye, requirement ke hisaab se 15-30 din ya zyada lag sakte hain.

Kya aapke websites mobile friendly hote hain?

Ji haan, humare sabhi websites 'fully responsive' hote hain, jo har device (mobile, tablet, desktop) par perfect dikhte hain.

Design mein changes kitni baar kar sakte hain?

Design approval stage tak major changes free hote hain. Ek baar development shuru hone ke baad, chote changes hum accommodate karte hain, lekin bade changes ke liye alag fee lag sakti hai.

Kya aap maintenance service bhi provide karte hain?

Ji haan, hum annual maintenance packages offer karte hain jisme content updates, security checks, aur technical support shamil hota hai. Pehle 3 months basic support free hota hai.

2. Domain & Hosting

Domain aur Hosting ka kharcha kitna aata hai?

Domain (.com/.in) aur basic hosting ka kharcha usually ₹1500 se ₹3000 prati varsh (per year) aata hai. Bade portals ke liye Cloud hosting ka charge zyada hota hai. Yeh service fee se alag hota hai.

Domain kiske naam se register hoga?

Domain hamesha **aapke naam** aur **aapke email** se register hoga. Iska pura control aapke paas rahega, hum sirf technical setup mein help karte hain.

Kya main apna existing hosting use kar sakta hoon?

Bilkul. Agar aapke paas pehle se hi hosting hai, toh hum uspar bhi website deploy kar denge. Lekin, hum quality aur speed ke liye apne recommended hosting (reseller store) se kharidne ki salah denge.

3. Payments & Refunds

Payment methods kya-kya hain?

Hum secure online payments (Razorpay Payment Links, UPI, Netbanking) aur offline bank transfer bhi accept karte hain. Cash/Cheque ke liye Contact Page dekhein.

Kya advance payment refundable hai?

Initial **Booking Amount** (Advance) **non-refundable** hota hai, kyunki isse hum design aur initial development shuru karte hain. Refund policy ki poori jaankari ke liye Refund Policy Page dekhein.

Payment fail ho gaya toh kya karein?

Agar online payment fail ho jaye ya amount cut ho jaaye, toh turant humare mobile number (+91 90658 85746) par call karein. Hum manual confirmation aur naye link ke liye help karenge.
